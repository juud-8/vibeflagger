VibeFlagger Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

Introduction

VibeFlagger ("we", "our", "the app") is a private relationship tracking application. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO personal data. All information you enter into VibeFlagger is stored ONLY on your device in a local encrypted SQLite database.

Data Storage

  • Location: All data stored locally on your iPhone
  • Encryption: Database protected by iOS security
  • Cloud Backup: Only if you have iCloud backup enabled for apps
  • Servers: We operate NO servers, NO cloud storage, NO data transmission

Permissions Used

Face ID / Touch ID

Purpose: Secure app lock authentication only
Data: Biometric data never leaves your device (handled by iOS)

Photo Library

Purpose: Saving PDF export reports only
Data: We only SAVE files, never READ your photos

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

Purpose: Process in-app purchase subscriptions
Data Shared: Anonymous purchase receipts only
Privacy: No personal information shared

Anthropic Claude AI (Premium Tier Only)

Purpose: Generate relationship behavior insights
Data Shared: Your relationship logs (anonymized, no personal identifiers)
Privacy: AI processes data in real-time, nothing stored on Anthropic servers
Opt-In: Only used when you explicitly request AI insights

Analytics & Tracking

We collect NO analytics. No tracking. No usage statistics. Nothing.

Your Rights

  • Delete Data: Simply delete the app - all data is permanently removed
  • Export Data: Use PDF export feature before deleting
  • No Account: No account required, no email collected, no login

Children's Privacy

VibeFlagger is rated 17+ and not intended for children under 17.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
